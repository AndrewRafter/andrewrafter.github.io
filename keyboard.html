Keyboard

Keyboards are very similar to piano but are more portable and versatile. They generally have less keys than a piano. Keyboards have a variety of effects built in to them which depends on the manufacturer and they can also be hooked up to pedals for even more effects.

Synthesizer Keyboard

Synthesizer Keyboards are very similar to regualar keyboards but the key difference is that they generate audio signals which are then converted to sound. Synthesizers can even imitate the sounds of other instruments
